LoG Meetup MidNorth, US 2023
Nov 18, 2023
University of Notre Dame, Indiana

What is this about?

In the context of the International Learning-on-Graph (LoG) Conference 2023, we are organising a Local Meetup in MidNorth US, on 18th November. It will be hosted in the University of Notre Dame. It will feature some keynote talks, debating as well as a poster session + a social event. This meetup aims to bring together the local Graph ML community, catalysing discussions and encourage more students to get involved.

Key information

  • 1. speaker invitation: You can register this form if you want to become a speaker

  • 2. participant invitation: You can just walk in. It would be better if you can register this form to help us estimate the number of attendence. We can offer some travel grant if possible

  • 3. What? A Graph ML Meetup for the LoG Conference 2023.

  • 4. Free to attend and open to everyone.

  • 5. When? 18th of November 2023, all day.

  • 6. Where? In Notre Dame.

  • 7. Objective: Connect the local graph learning community in the region.

  • 8. Note: We are supported by the Learning-on-Graphs conference 2023, Snap Inc, and NSF funding
