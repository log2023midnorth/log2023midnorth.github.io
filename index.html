LoG Meetup MidNorth, US 2023
Jan 14, 2024
University of Notre Dame, Indiana

What is this about?

In conjuction with the 2023 Learning on Graphs (LoG) Conference, we are organizing a local meetup in the Mid-North US. The meetup will take place on the Jan 14th. It will be hosted by the University of Notre Dame. The meetup will feature keynote talks, a panel discussion, as well as a poster session and a social event. This meetup aims to bring together the local Graph ML community, fostering discussions and collaborations while encouraging more students to get involved.

Key information

  • 1. What? A Graph ML Meetup for the LoG Conference 2024.

  • 2. Where? 303 Cushing Hall of Engineering. Parking location: 18780 Holy Cross Dr, Notre Dame, IN 46556.

  • 3. When? 14th of January 2024, all day.

  • 4. Objective: Connect the local graph learning community in the region.

  • Note: We are supported by the Learning-on-Graphs conference 2023 and NSF funding
